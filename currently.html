Writing

I refer to my WIPs by the name of their leading lady, so right now I am in the process of wrapping up Chelsea. Chelsea is a twenty-two year old recent college grad struggling to navigate new adulthood. To help combat her student loan debt and growing list of bills she gets a second job working as an assistant to the chaotic organizer of Found, a non-profit after school program. Between the jobs, having fun with her friends, and trying to figure out who she is out from the shadow of her impressive siblings, the last thing Chelsea wants is a boyfriend tying her down. She just can't help falling for Lee, the sweet volunteer that she always seems to be bumping into. But can they make it work when everything in her life seems to be working against them?
Chelsea's story explores family, both blood and found, friendship, and first real love.
